Data Mesh & Data Products

Data Product

  • data product: unit of architecture that can be independently deployed with high functional cohesion, and includes all the structural elements required for its function.
  • a shift in mindset from data as a byproduct of transactional systems and processes to data purposefully designed and packaged as a “product” for an analytical consumption
  • Operational vs Analytical
